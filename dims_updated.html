Drug Information Management System

Medicine Name Uses Side Effects
Paracetamol Pain relief, fever reduction. Liver damage (overuse), allergic reactions.
Amoxicillin Antibiotic for bacterial infections. Diarrhea, nausea, rash, allergic reactions.
Ibuprofen Pain relief, anti-inflammatory, fever reduction. Stomach upset, dizziness, ulcers, kidney damage (long-term use).
Metformin For managing type 2 diabetes. Stomach upset, nausea, diarrhea, lactic acidosis (rare).
Ciprofloxacin Antibiotic for various infections. Nausea, diarrhea, tendonitis, heart problems (rare).
Amlodipine For high blood pressure and angina. Swelling, dizziness, headache, low blood pressure.
Esomeprazole For acid reflux, GERD, ulcers. Headache, diarrhea, nausea, kidney issues (rare).
Pantoprazole For acid reflux, GERD, ulcers. Headache, nausea, abdominal pain, vitamin B12 deficiency (long-term use).
Diclofenac Pain relief, anti-inflammatory for arthritis and muscle pain. Stomach pain, ulcers, gastrointestinal bleeding, kidney damage (long-term use).
Folic Acid For pregnancy support, folate deficiency. Nausea, flatulence (rare), can mask vitamin B12 deficiency.
Losartan For high blood pressure and heart failure. Dizziness, fatigue, high potassium levels, kidney problems (rare).
Clopidogrel To prevent blood clots. Bleeding, bruising, severe bleeding (rare), liver problems.
Salbutamol For asthma and other breathing issues. Shaky hands, increased heart rate, headache, muscle cramps (rare).
Ranitidine For acid reflux and ulcers. Headache, dizziness, constipation, liver problems (rare).
Cetirizine For allergies (e.g., hay fever, rashes, sneezing). Drowsiness, dry mouth, headache, allergic reactions (rare).
Lisinopril For high blood pressure and heart failure. Dizziness, cough, high potassium levels, kidney problems (rare).
Hydrochlorothiazide Diuretic for high blood pressure and fluid retention. Increased urination, dizziness, dehydration, allergic reactions (rare).
Fluoxetine For depression, anxiety, OCD. Nausea, sleep disturbances, sexual dysfunction, weight changes, suicidal thoughts (rare).
Ondansetron For preventing nausea and vomiting (e.g., chemotherapy, surgery). Headache, constipation, dizziness, heart rhythm changes (rare).
Tamsulosin For enlarged prostate (BPH) to help urination. Dizziness, headache, abnormal ejaculation, low blood pressure (rare).
